About Me

You may ask, "What does a bike connected to a shopping cart have to do with web development?"
Well, as you know, web development is all about solving problems. The problem can be figuring out how to most effectively advertise your business, or it can be finding a way to talk with your far-flung friends and family, but that root cause is always there.
I took this picture because I recognized, that like McGyver, the crew of and team behind Apollo 13 (who had to figure out how to literally fit a square peg in a round hole with supplies available on the spacecraft), and the creator of this bike-cart, (who clearly needed a find a way to carry a lot of stuff around):

  1. It is always possible to "improvise, adapt, and overcome"
  2. If it's stupid and it works, it's not stupid
My name is Daniel Juarez, and I'm an entry-level web developer ready and eager to help your company solve problems, even if that means building a bike-cart. Come get to know me!
